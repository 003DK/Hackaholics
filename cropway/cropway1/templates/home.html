Key Government Initiatives for Farmers

Stay informed about the crucial government schemes designed to support the agricultural sector. We provide easy-to-understand details about eligibility and benefits to help you access the resources you need.

  • PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi): A direct income support scheme providing financial assistance to all landholding farmer families across the country. Eligibility: All landholding farmer families. Benefit:₹6,000 per year, payable in three equal installments of ₹2,000 every four months.
  • RKVY (Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana): A state-led scheme focused on promoting agri-infrastructure development and innovation in agriculture and allied sectors. Eligibility: Projects proposed by State Governments. Benefit: Varies based on the project and state-specific priorities.
  • Agri-Startup Funding (Various Schemes): Initiatives aimed at providing financial support and mentorship to startups in the agriculture technology space. Eligibility: Registered startups with innovative solutions in agriculture. Benefit: Funding amounts can range from ₹15 lakhs to ₹1 crore or more, depending on the scheme and stage of the startup.
  • Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY): A crop insurance scheme to provide financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage arising out of natural calamities, pests & diseases. Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas. Benefit: Coverage varies depending on the crop and sum insured.

Explore the full range of government schemes and application processes through official portals linked on our resources page.
